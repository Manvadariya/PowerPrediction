About Us

The Delhi Power Prediction System is designed to analyze and forecast electricity demand across different sectors in Delhi. It helps in optimizing power distribution, preventing grid failures, and ensuring efficient load management. Key Features: Accurate Demand Forecasting: Predicts electricity consumption patterns using historical and real-time data. Sector-Based Analysis: Differentiates between residential, corporate, and industrial power consumption for better resource allocation. Compensation Strategies: Provides methods to balance supply-demand fluctuations and mitigate power instability. Interactive Data Visualization: Displays detailed graphs and geographical maps for better understanding of power usage trends. Role-Based Access: Allows users like Normal Users, Government Engineers, Admins, and Researchers to interact with the system based on their roles. This system empowers energy authorities, researchers, and consumers with data-driven insights to ensure a stable and efficient power supply for Delhi.
